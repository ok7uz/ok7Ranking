ok7Rankings (https://ok7-rankings-91ee2cd70e74.herokuapp.com) is a site that provides real-time live FIFA Men's World Ranking based on FIFA Men's Ranking Procedure. Through this site you will get information about:
  • Current FIFA Men's World Ranking (Updated daily).
  • The current ranking of each confederation is formed separately (UEFA, CAF, CONMEBOL, CONCACAF, AFC, OFC).
  • The page of each national team and information about the points gained or lost in the last matches.
  • Information about points gained or lost by national teams in the last matches.
  • Fixtures and winning odds based on FIFA points.
  • The statistics are about the teams that have made the biggest increase or decrease in the last games.
  • Information about how many FIFA points the teams will earn or lose in the upcoming matches (Calculator)
